About TARDIS

Welcome to TARDIS, an organization dedicated to preserving the rich local history of our communities. We believe that understanding our past is essential to building a better future.

Our mission is to research, document, and share the stories of the people, places, and events that have shaped our local area. We offer guided tours, educational programs, and a wealth of online resources to bring history to life.
